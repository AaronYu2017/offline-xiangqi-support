FAQ

  • Can I use the app without internet? Yes. The app works fully offline and supports Airplane Mode.
  • What game modes are available? Local two-player mode and AI mode.
  • Which devices are supported? iPhone and iPad.
  • Which languages are supported? Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and English.
